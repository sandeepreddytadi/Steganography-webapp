Image Steganography

Using Midpoint Transformation Technique

Project Overview

This project explores the field of image steganography, focusing on the Midpoint Transformation Technique as a means of securely hiding information within digital images. Our aim is to enhance data security by concealing sensitive data in images, making it difficult for unauthorized individuals to detect or retrieve the hidden information.
